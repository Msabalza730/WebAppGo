Problem Statement

Nowadays, many dogs live in the street in the middle of nowhere. Some dogs do not have a home and are abandoned, others, because they are not of a specific breed of dogs, these dogs born and raised in the street so they have to manage to survive, find their food, and often drink stagnant water.

Justification

Stray dogs tend to suffer greater diseases due to lack of hygiene and a good diet, for this reason CanApp is born.
